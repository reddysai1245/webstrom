AWARDS AND ACCOLADES

The Longest Painting

The Longest Painting measured 2,235m (7332' 8") and was created by students from the Department of Architecture in Chennai, India on 11th January 2003.

Birla White Yuvaratna

Birla White Yuvaratna has awarded the Department of Architecture for sending the maximum number of entries at the National Level design competition.

Limca Book of Records

Limca Book of Records has considered the record for the Longest Maze created by the students using recycled materials during Apologue 2013 - Zonal Nasa Convention hosted by the Department of Architecture, Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, for the year 2014.

National level Award

National level Awardby Sharon Plywoods for the Best ‘Sustainable Office Design’ workshop was awarded to the Department of Architecture, Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology in 2013.

NIASA Thesis Awards

The Department of Architecture was also awarded the Certificate of Appreciation for hosting the NIASA Thesis Awards - 2011 conducted by National Institute of Advanced Studies in Architecture.

Pidilite Award for Excellencewas

Pidilite Award for Excellencewas awarded to the Department in the years 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2012


Professor Tony Memorial Award

Professor Tony Memorial Award was awarded to the for Excellence in Academic Designs, at the Zonal NASA Convention - 2006

Best Studio Project

The Department of Architecture was also awarded the Best Studio Projectfor the fourth year academic design by Saint Gobain during Transparence 2011 and 2013.
